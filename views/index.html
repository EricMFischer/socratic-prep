WELCOME TO SOCRATIC PREP

How We Teach

Socratic Prep has a comprehensive list of strategies for both the SAT and ACT, reflective of the concepts tested on each exam. Our focus is to make our material as concise as possible, so students have more success remembering and practically applying the strategies. Our instructors have almost a decade of experience at the most elite test prep companies, have 99% scores on both the SAT and ACT, and went on to study at great universities. We have higher standards than other test prep companies we left, so consider this a bootcamp from the very best.

CLEAR STRATEGIES

We take pride in being short and sweet with the strategies, so you can understand them 100% and simply focus on applying them during the exam.

PRACTICAL APPROACH

Most companies teach you and just hope that you correctly apply the curriculum. We have strategies associated with different types of SAT/ACT questions, so it is very clear when to apply your knowledge.

ONE ON ONE TUTORING

We only offer one on one tutoring, so that we can pinpoint the concepts and strategies you are weak on. Homework review is when you'll grow most as a student.

INSTRUCTORS

OUR COURSES

We have two courses, an SAT and ACT course. Each course has 12 lessons that are 3 hours each for a total of 36 hours. For each lesson, there is a slide deck that the Instructor will email to a student afterwards for review. All lessons are structured the same: a review of homework followed by a deep-dive into new strategies. Lessons take place over Skype, where the Instructor will guide a student through the slide deck and offer very important Study Guide Notes for each strategy. Course Materials are listed below:

SAT/ACT Study Guide

When you sign up for a course, your instructor will email you his/her skype name and an SAT or ACT Study Guide. You will add critical reminders for each strategy to this study guide.

Official SAT/ACT Book

For homework, we only assign official SAT or ACT test sections. Insanely enough, some companies don't give you official SAT/ACT questions for homework practice. For the SAT, please have the The Official SAT Study Guide (2016). For the ACT, please have the The Real ACT Prep Guide (3rd edition).

Slide Decks for 12 Lessons

Your instructor will have the relevant day's slide deck and will assign homework from those slides. It's required that you do the homework on paper and are prepared to review it the next lesson.
